Traffic Lights

Your Mission

  • Optimize traffic flow
  • Avoid killing people

Library: gpiozero


            from gpiozero import TrafficLights, Button

            lights = TrafficLights(21, 20, 16)
            button = Button(5)

API: TrafficLights


            lights.on()
            lights.off()
            lights.red.on()
            lights.amber.on()
            lights.green.off()
            lights.value = (1, 1, 0)

API: Button


            def my_func():
                print("Don't push the button!")

            button.when_pressed = my_func
