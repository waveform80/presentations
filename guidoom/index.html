Conclusion - Toolkits

  • GTK's very cool … but QT's designer kicks the crap out of Glade
  • Also, without (decent, well packaged) Windows support, GTK3 isn't really x-platform
  • QT seems to be the toolkit of choice of the scientific community
  • Linux is still way easier to install stuff on
  • But Ubuntu's matplotlib desperately needs updating
  • (yes, there's PPAs for it, but you try explaining add-apt-repository and apt-get update to scientists …)
  • Maybe I just tried this at the wrong time; it seems the two major toolkits are in a transitional period
